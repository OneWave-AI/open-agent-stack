aurora-mesh

Soft futuristic surfaces that drift in teal, amber, and rose

A dark, low-contrast theme built on animated gradient-mesh light, glassy low-opacity cards, and ambient blurred orbs. Motion never fully settles — it breathes. Apply the theme with data-theme="aurora-mesh".

Mesh background

Layered radial gradients drift on a 24s loop behind a heavy blur, producing soft moving light.

Glass surfaces

Low-opacity fills with backdrop blur and hairline borders keep panels light and translucent.

Gradient fades

Dividers and edges dissolve into transparency rather than drawing hard lines.

Body text sits at a comfortable 7.9:1 contrast in muted tone, while primary text reaches 15.6:1 — both clear AA+ on the deep base canvas.
