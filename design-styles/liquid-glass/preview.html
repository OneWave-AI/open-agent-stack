Design Style

Liquid Glass

Frosted, refractive surfaces float over deep slate. One high-vibrancy teal accent carries the energy; light edges and soft glow give every panel translucent depth.

01 / surface

Frosted Depth

Translucent containers blur whatever drifts behind them, catching a thin light edge along the top to read as real glass.

02 / accent

Teal Charge

A single vibrant accent anchors focus, active states, and the glow that pools beneath each surface on hover.

03 / motion

Springy Lift

Hover any panel: it rises with a spring overshoot and gains a teal glow, the signature parallax of the system.
