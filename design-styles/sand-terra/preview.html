Design Style — sand-terra

Warm editorial-organic, built for the dark.

Sand and terracotta over near-black. Low-to-mid contrast, soft film grain, rounded containers with warm diffuse shadows, and motion that rises and settles slowly. A serif speaks; a humanist sans does the work.

Fraunces display Inter body JetBrains Mono

Containers

Generous corner radius and a warm, diffuse shadow lift each surface off the base without hard edges. Hover to feel the slow ease.

Hairlines

Dividers and borders stay near the background — structure you sense more than see, keeping the composition calm and editorial.

Body copy holds a comfortable measure at --leading-normal with muted sand for secondary text. Accent terracotta carries calls to action and links while staying AA-legible on the near-black base.

Apply by adding data-theme="sand-terra" to a root element and importing theme.css. For Tailwind, spread tailwind.tokens.js into theme.extend.
