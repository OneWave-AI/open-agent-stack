# ai-news-hq — Source List

Researched June 2026. Sources are grouped by job so the `analyst` can weight them differently.

## Model releases (real-time, highest priority)
- **LLM Stats** — `llm-stats.com/llm-updates` and `/ai-news`. Real-time tracking of GPT/o-series, Claude, Gemini, Llama, Mistral, DeepSeek releases with dates, capabilities, and benchmarks.

## High-cadence feed
- **MarkTechPost** — `marktechpost.com/feed/`. Broad coverage of releases and paper roundups; the single strongest RSS feed.

## Daily digests
- **TLDR AI** — engineer-friendly bullets, weekdays.
- **Techpresso** — broad daily coverage.

## Weekly deep dives
- **The Batch** (Andrew Ng / DeepLearning.AI) — best free technical read.
- **Import AI** (Jack Clark) — research + policy analysis.

## Community signal
- **Hacker News** — methodology debates when a model ships or a paper drops.
- **r/LocalLLaMA** — open-source / local model scene, quantizations, hardware.

## Editorial (longer-horizon)
- **MIT Technology Review** — fact-checked, researcher commentary.

## Vendor primary sources
- OpenAI News, Anthropic News, Google DeepMind, Meta AI — for first-party release posts.

## Ranking guidance
Weight model releases and first-party posts highest, corroborate community buzz against a primary source before including, and always flag items that touch the reader's declared stack.
