# Ranking Rubric

Score each verified candidate 0-100 by summing weighted criterion contributions, then sort descending.

## Weights
| Criterion | Max | What it measures |
|---|---|---|
| Relevance | 35 | How directly the item concerns the topic and its core subtopics. |
| Impact | 25 | Scale and materiality of consequences (markets, users, policy, technology). |
| Freshness | 15 | Recency within the lookback window. |
| Source credibility | 15 | Primary source > established outlet > aggregator. |
| Novelty | 10 | New information vs. a restatement of known facts. |

## Scoring Bands

### Relevance (0-35)
- 30-35: Squarely about the topic; topic is the subject of the item.
- 18-29: Topic is a major theme but shares focus with adjacent subjects.
- 6-17: Topic appears as context or a passing mention.
- 0-5: Tangential; consider dropping.

### Impact (0-25)
- 20-25: Broad, material effect (major launch, regulation, large funding, outage, M&A).
- 12-19: Notable effect on a segment or region.
- 5-11: Incremental update or single-company minor news.
- 0-4: Negligible.

### Freshness (0-15)
- 13-15: Published within the most recent third of the window.
- 8-12: Middle third.
- 3-7: Oldest third but still inside the window.
- 0-2: At the window edge or update-only.

### Source credibility (0-15)
- 13-15: Primary source (official release, filing, first-party post).
- 9-12: Established, reputable outlet with bylined reporting.
- 4-8: Secondary outlet or syndicated coverage.
- 0-3: Aggregator, unbylined, or low-trust source. Verify before inclusion.

### Novelty (0-10)
- 8-10: Reveals genuinely new information.
- 4-7: Adds meaningful detail to a known story.
- 0-3: Rehash of already-reported facts.

## Tie-Breaking
1. Higher freshness.
2. Higher source credibility.
3. Higher relevance.

## Worked Examples

### Example A — official product launch (topic: "AI agents")
- Relevance 34, Impact 22, Freshness 14, Source credibility 15, Novelty 9 = 94. Top of list.

### Example B — established outlet recaps a known funding round
- Relevance 28, Impact 14, Freshness 9, Source credibility 11, Novelty 4 = 66. Mid list.

### Example C — aggregator restates a rumor, no primary confirmation
- Relevance 20, Impact 10, Freshness 10, Source credibility 3, Novelty 5 = 48, but unverified. Move to Watch, exclude from ranked list.
